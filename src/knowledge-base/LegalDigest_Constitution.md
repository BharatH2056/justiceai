# Legal Digest: Constitution of India (As of May 2024)

## Part III: Fundamental Rights (Arts. 12–35)

* **Art. 14**: Equality before law and equal protection of laws.
* **Art. 15**: Prohibition of discrimination on grounds of religion, race, caste, sex, or place of birth.
* **Art. 16**: Equality of opportunity in public employment.
* **Art. 19**: Protection of certain rights regarding freedom of speech, assembly, association, movement, residence, and profession.
* **Art. 21**: Protection of life and personal liberty. (Includes Right to Housing, Environment, and Privacy).
* **Art. 21A**: Right to free and compulsory education for children (6–14 years).
* **Art. 22**: Protection against arrest and detention in certain cases.
* **Art. 32**: Right to move the Supreme Court for enforcement of rights (Writ Jurisdiction: Habeas Corpus, Mandamus, Prohibition, Quo Warranto, Certiorari).

## Part IV: Directive Principles of State Policy (Arts. 36–51)

* **Art. 38**: State to secure a social order for the promotion of welfare.
* **Art. 39A**: **Equal Justice and Free Legal Aid.** Required to ensure justice is not denied by reason of economic or other disabilities.
* **Art. 44**: Uniform Civil Code for citizens.
* **Art. 51**: Promotion of international peace and security.

## Part IV-A: Fundamental Duties (Art. 51A)

* Rights are accompanied by duties, including upholding the sovereignty of India and safeguarding public property.

## Part V & VI: The Judiciary (Arts. 124, 214, 226)

* **Art. 124**: Establishment and constitution of the Supreme Court.
* **Art. 214**: High Courts for States.
* **Art. 226**: **Power of High Courts to issue certain writs.** Broader scope than Art. 32, as it covers enforcement of Part III rights AND any other purpose.

## Important Amendments (Recent)

* **101st Amendment (2016)**: Introduction of GST.
* **103rd Amendment (2019)**: 10% reservation for Economically Weaker Sections (EWS).
* **105th Amendment (2021)**: Restoring powers of State Govts to identify OBCs.
* **106th Amendment (2023)**: **Nari Shakti Vandan Adhiniyam.** Reserves 1/3rd of seats for women in Lok Sabha and State Legislative Assemblies.
